# Biohacking-Based Learning Optimization

## 1. Circadian Rhythm (概日リズム)
- **Concept**: The internal 24-hour clock that regulates alertness and sleep-wake cycles.
- **Application**:
    - **Light Management**: Morning sunlight exposure (high lux) to reset the clock and improve daytime alertness. Avoiding blue light 90-120 mins before bed.
    - **Timing**: Scheduling cognitively demanding tasks during "Peak Alertness" windows (usually mid-morning for most).
    - **Temperature**: Keeping the study environment cool/neutral and sleep environment specifically cool (16-19°C) to promote deep sleep.

## 2. Glucose Monitoring (血糖値管理)
- **Concept**: Stable blood sugar is critical for sustained mental energy and preventing "brain fog."
- **Application**:
    - **Glucose Spikes**: Using CGM (Continuous Glucose Monitors) to identify foods that cause energy crashes.
    - **Meal Order**: Eating fiber and protein before carbohydrates to flatten the glucose curve.
    - **Post-Meal Activity**: Light movement (walking) for 10-15 mins after eating to improve insulin sensitivity and maintain energy.

## 3. Best Practices
- **Hydration**: Even slight dehydration significantly impairs cognitive function.
- **Strategic Supplementation**: L-Theanine (with caffeine) for focused alertness without jitters, or Magnesium for sleep quality.
- **Sleep Hygiene**: Consistency in wake-up times to stabilize biological cycles.
